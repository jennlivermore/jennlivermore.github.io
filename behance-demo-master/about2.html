Howdy!

I’m currently a post-baccalaureate graphic design student at MICA (Maryland Institute College of Art). Before venturing into graphic design, I got a degree in Environmental Analysis from Scripps College and then spent a year in San Francisco working on a park-planning project. I’ve also lived in Salt Lake City, Australia, and Denmark.

Besides graphic design, I get excited about fresh produce and cooking, photogenic dogs, sea water, thunderstorm skies, and hiking. I’ve been collecting rocks, shells, and fragments of print for as long as I can remember (but in a neat, organized, non-hoarding way).
